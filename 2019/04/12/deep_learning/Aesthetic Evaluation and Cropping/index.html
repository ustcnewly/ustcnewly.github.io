Aesthetic Evaluation and Cropping

Posted on 2019-04-12 | In paper note

Aesthetic evaluation: mimick photographic rules (e.g., golden ratio, the rule of thirds, color harmonies [3]), preserve important content, and consider object/human-object/human interaction. Here is a book about aesthetic design.
